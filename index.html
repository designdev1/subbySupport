Privacy Policy for Subby — Subscription Tracker

Effective Date: 1 April 2025

1. Data Collection

Subby does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users.

2. Data Usage

As no data is collected, there is no data to use, share, or sell.

3. Third-Party Services

Subby uses ExchangeRate-API (exchangerate-api.com) to fetch the latest currency exchange rates. This helps keep subscription cost conversions accurate.

No data is ever sent from your device—Subby only retrieves exchange rate information and does not transmit any user information or analytics. Everything stays on your device.

4. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users of any significant changes.
